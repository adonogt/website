About the Studio

I am an embedded systems developer dedicated to blending thoughtful hardware architecture with robust firmware. My approach is calm, precise, and informed by years of building products where every byte and every trace matters.

Philosophy

Embedded work thrives on discipline and clarity. I prioritize readable code, well-documented schematics, and transparent communication with collaborators. The goal is always the same: systems that feel effortless to operate because the complexity is handled with care behind the scenes.

Experience Highlights

I design and implement firmware across microcontroller platforms, lead PCB layout efforts, and guide teams toward maintainable workflows. Toolchains like KiCad, Altium, Neovim, and Doom Emacs enable rapid iteration without sacrificing precision.

Values

Minimalism is not an aesthetic choice alone—it is a promise to keep the focus on what truly matters. Every interface, document, and code review is an opportunity to remove friction and invite clarity.
